Human Resources Development and Innovation (HRDI) Guidelines

Introduction
HRDI is committed to fostering employee growth and organizational excellence through comprehensive training and development programs.

Employee Development Programs

1. Onboarding Program
All new employees must complete a 2-week onboarding program that includes:
- Company culture and values training
- Department-specific orientation
- IT systems and tools training
- Health and safety compliance

2. Continuous Learning
Employees are encouraged to pursue continuous learning through:
- Monthly skill-building workshops
- Online learning platforms (LinkedIn Learning, Coursera)
- Professional certification support
- Quarterly leadership seminars

3. Career Development
HRDI supports career growth through:
- Annual performance reviews
- Individual development plans (IDPs)
- Internal job posting system
- Mentorship programs

Leave Policies

Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 15 days of annual leave per year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum of 5 consecutive days without supervisor approval

Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually
- Medical certificate required for absences over 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave does not roll over to the next year

Parental Leave
- Primary caregivers: 12 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregivers: 4 weeks paid leave
- Must notify HR at least 4 weeks before expected leave date

Performance Management

Performance Reviews
- Conducted bi-annually (June and December)
- Based on SMART goals set at the beginning of each period
- Include self-assessment and manager evaluation
- Results influence promotion and compensation decisions

Performance Improvement Plans
- Issued when performance falls below expectations
- 90-day improvement period with specific milestones
- Regular check-ins with supervisor and HR
- Failure to improve may result in termination

Innovation Initiatives

Innovation Lab
- Dedicated space for creative collaboration
- Available for all employees to test new ideas
- Monthly innovation challenges with prizes
- Partnerships with local universities and startups

Idea Submission Process
1. Submit ideas through the Innovation Portal
2. Initial screening by innovation committee
3. Selected ideas receive funding and resources
4. Quarterly presentations to leadership

Employee Benefits

Health Insurance
- Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Covers employee and immediate family members
- Low co-pays and deductibles
- Wellness program incentives

Retirement Plan
- 401(k) with company match up to 6%
- Vesting period: 3 years
- Financial planning services available

Work-Life Balance
- Flexible work arrangements available
- Remote work options for eligible positions
- Gym membership reimbursement
- Employee assistance program (EAP)
