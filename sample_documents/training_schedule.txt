Registration Process
1. Visit the HRDI Portal at hrdi.company.com
2. Navigate to Training Calendar
3. Select desired training program
4. Complete registration form
5. Obtain supervisor approval
6. Receive confirmation email

Cancellation Policy
- Cancellations must be made at least 5 business days before the training start date
- Late cancellations may result in training fee charges
- No-shows will be charged the full training fee

Training Attendance Policy
- Attendance is mandatory for all registered sessions
- Participants must attend at least 90% of training hours to receive certification
- Training materials will be provided on the first day
- Certificates of completion issued within 2 weeks after training
